## Reaching out

The best way to reach me is by email at [quentin.fournier@mila.quebec](mailto:quentin.fournier@mila.quebec). I read every message I receive but can be slow to respond, so feel free to nudge me after a week. Our lab is based at [Mila](https://mila.quebec/en) in Montréal.

## Joining the lab

I am recruiting PhD students and MSc and undergraduate interns on a rolling basis to work on protein language models, biological sequence modeling, and AI for drug discovery. A strong background in machine learning is required, and experience in computational biology is a plus. If you are interested in joining, please email me your CV, a brief summary of your research interests, and your transcripts.

## Collaborations

I welcome academic and industry collaborations on protein language models, biological sequence modeling, and AI for drug discovery. If you are interested in collaborating, please send me an email with a concise description of your project, including what you are working on, what you would like to explore, and how you see us contributing.
