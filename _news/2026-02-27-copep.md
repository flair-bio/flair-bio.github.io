New preprint: [**CoPeP: Benchmarking Continual Pretraining for Protein Language Models**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.00253). A benchmark for studying how protein language models can keep up with new data without retraining from scratch. With Darshan Patil, Pranshu Malviya, Mathieu Reymond, and Sarath Chandar. In collaboration with Genentech.
